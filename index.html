Questions?

What makes a lead "qualified"?

We score each business on a 7-point system: no website (+2), active social media (+2), low reviews indicating early stage (+1), booking service needed (+1), and inconsistent branding (+1). Only businesses scoring 3+ make the list.

What niches do you cover?

Barbershops, nail salons, hair stylists, lash techs, estheticians, photographers, fitness trainers, tattoo artists, car detailing, and cleaning services. More niches added based on demand.

How fresh are the leads?

All leads are verified within 7 days of delivery. We check that businesses are still active, contact info is current, and they haven't launched a website since we found them.

Can I request specific locations?

Yes. All plans let you specify city/metro area. Business plan also supports ZIP code targeting and radius-based searches.
